Wildlife: Nature's Untamed Beauty

Explore Earth's incredible wild creatures and their habitats

What is Wildlife?

Wildlife includes all undomesticated animals and plants living in natural ecosystems — from vast jungles to open oceans.

Diversity of Wildlife

From elephants to eagles, deserts to rainforests — wildlife comes in endless shapes and forms.

Why Wildlife Matters

Wildlife balances ecosystems, supports agriculture, and connects us to nature’s rhythms.

Threats to Wildlife

Climate change, poaching, and deforestation are driving countless species to extinction every year.

Protecting Wildlife

Preserves, laws, eco-tourism, and education help ensure wild species survive and thrive.
